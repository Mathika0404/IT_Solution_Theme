FAQ

Answers to common questions about our IT solutions and services

How much will it cost?

+

To estimate the cost, we first understand your project requirements, features, and design needs. After an initial discussion, we provide a ballpark estimate followed by a detailed quote.

How long does it take to design a project?

+

Project timelines depend on complexity and content availability. Small projects may take a few weeks, while larger systems take longer.

How can I associate with your IT company?

+

Simply contact us with your requirements. After discussion, we suggest the best engagement model and start development once finalized.

Can you improve my existing website?

+

Yes. We can redesign your current website to give it a modern, professional look with improved performance and user experience.

What are your payment terms?

+

Payments are usually divided into milestones—initial, mid-project, and final delivery. We accept online payments and major credit cards.

Yes. We offer ongoing support and maintenance services including bug fixes, performance optimization, security updates, and feature enhancements to ensure your IT solution runs smoothly after launch.
